# Tweet Style Guide

## Principles

- Reduced, restrained, minimal — let the reader feel the impact
- Short declarative sentences. No filler words
- Show, don't label. Never end with "Incredible" or "Amazing" — let the reader arrive there
- State what happened plainly. The facts are the story
- Braun / Japanese architecture energy: every word earns its place

## Example

> Redesigned my personal website this morning. Started on my phone while brushing my teeth. Live before I got to work. Never opened GitHub or touched code.

## Anti-patterns

- Over-explaining the process
- Superlatives and self-congratulation
- Starting with "This morning I had an idea to..."
- Telling the reader how to feel
