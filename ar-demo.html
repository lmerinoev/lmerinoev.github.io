Augmented Reality Experience

Experience interactive augmented reality with gesture controls. This demo uses AR.js to create immersive 3D experiences that respond to touch and gesture interactions.

Mobile Recommended: This AR experience works best on mobile devices with cameras. Please use your phone or tablet for the full experience.
How to Use the AR Demo
  • Allow camera access when prompted
  • Tap anywhere on the screen to place 3D objects
  • Use pinch gestures to scale objects
  • Drag to move objects around
  • Use the reset button to clear all objects
AR Controls
Gesture Controls
Interact with 3D objects using natural touch gestures for scaling, rotation, and movement.
3D Objects
Place and manipulate various 3D shapes in your real-world environment.
Mobile Optimized
Designed for mobile devices with camera access for the best AR experience.
Real-time Tracking
Objects stay anchored in 3D space as you move your device around.
