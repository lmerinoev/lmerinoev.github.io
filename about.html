It's Me Luis

I'm Luis Merino, a human-AI interaction researcher with a passion for understanding trust formation in collaborative systems. I have a deep fascination with the intersection of artificial intelligence, human psychology, and decision-making processes. My main goal is to bridge the divide between humans and intelligent systems, transforming complex AI challenges into meaningful and seamless collaborative experiences.

More About Me

Luis Merino holds advanced degrees in Human-Computer Interaction and has a relentless drive for staying up-to-date with the latest AI technologies and research methodologies. Actively involved in the research community, Luis regularly participates in diverse conferences and workshops focused on human-AI collaboration.

When he's not immersed in research work, he finds solace in photography, exploring new coffee shops, and building interactive demos that showcase the future of human-machine partnerships. His work has been published in top-tier conferences and journals, contributing to our understanding of trust in AI systems.
