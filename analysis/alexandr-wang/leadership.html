Leadership Style

The Architect-Operator

Wang operates in two distinct modes that rarely coexist in a single leader. The tension between them is productive, but carries inherent risk.

The Architect

Designs organizations from first principles. Spent seven months building foundations at Meta before shipping anything. Approached MSL as a "blank slate" org design problem.

"How do you set something up such that it has durability and a differential point of view all the way down."

The Operator

Deeply hands-on. Reviews every hire. QC'd data personally at Scale AI. Described as "personally pained" by customer unhappiness. Classic founder mode.

The productive tension: The architect demands patience; the operator demands urgency. Foundation-building requires time, but operator instincts push for immediate results. The risk is these two modes pulling against each other.

Hiring Philosophy

Wang wrote a blog post titled "Hire People Who Give a Damn." The title is the thesis.

"You can tell people who phone it in versus people who hang on to their work... it's so incredibly monumental and forceful and important to them."

  • Reviews every hire personally
  • Intrinsic motivation over credentials
  • Talent density over headcount
  • Looks for people whose relationship to their work is visceral and observable

Organizational Design

Research-product tandem

No handoffs between research and product. They work together from the start.

"Biggest breakthroughs come from researchers thinking about product and product people thinking about research."

Remove artificial deadlines

"Building at the best pace" rather than mortgaging long-term opportunity for short-term calendar pressure.

Philosophy embedded at every level

Culture is not a poster on the wall. Wang wants a "differential point of view all the way down" into how the team thinks, hires, and operates daily.

Designed from a blank slate

Built MSL for "highest talent density" and "best possible scientific foundations." Every seat matters. The org chart is a design artifact.
